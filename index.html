Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2025

1. Introduction

NeuroFlux ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the App, including:

  • Mental Health Data: Descriptions of your mental health concerns, life aspects, and personal reflections that you choose to share for analysis
  • Mood Entries: Daily mood scores and optional comments you record in the journal
  • Calendar Events: Events and schedules you create within the App
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App's features

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics
  • Subscription status and purchase history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide AI-powered mental health analysis and recommendations
  • Generate personalized life aspect assessments
  • Enable AI Coach conversations
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Process subscription payments
  • Provide customer support

4. AI Processing

Your mental health data and journal entries are processed by OpenAI's API (GPT-4o-mini) to generate personalized insights and recommendations. This processing occurs through our secure backend server. We do not share your personal information with OpenAI beyond what is necessary for providing the service.

5. Data Storage

Local Storage: Most of your data (mental health analysis, journal entries, calendar events) is stored locally on your device using iOS's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to sync it.

Backend Processing: When you use AI features, your input is temporarily sent to our backend server for processing. This data is not stored permanently on our servers.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • API keys and sensitive credentials are stored securely
  • Data is stored locally on your device when possible
  • We use industry-standard security practices

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered analysis and chat features. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store: For subscription payments. See Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Railway: For hosting our backend services. See Railway's Privacy Policy

8. Subscription Information

Subscription payments are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing in accordance with their privacy policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the App
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the App (local data will be removed)
  • Opt out of data collection (though this may limit App functionality)
  • Request information about how your data is used

10. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the App
  • Backend Processing: Data sent for AI processing is not stored permanently
  • Subscription Data: Retained as required by law and for service provision

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
